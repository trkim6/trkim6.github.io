Amusement Parks

  • Disneyland Tokyo - Enjoy Disneyland in Japan! See your favorite characters and watch special shows in the park.
  • Universal Studios Japan - Universal Studios Japan has the Super Nintendo World, as well as a couple of rides exclusive to Universal Studios Japan.
  • Sanrio Puroland - A theme park based on characters like Hello Kitty, Kuromi, and Cinnamoroll. This is a fully indoor amusement park with rides, food, shows, and shops.
