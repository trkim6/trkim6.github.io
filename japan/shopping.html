Shopping in Japan

  • GINZASIX - A 6-level shopping mall in Ginza filled with designer brands and luxury goods. This shopping mall has a rooftop garden where you can eat as well as a food court inside.
  • HARAKADO - A shopping mall in Harajuku. This mall is great for shopping for a younger demographic.
  • Makuharishintoshin-Aeonmall - Many department stores to shop at in this mall. There are a bunch of food options and a wide variety of stores to shop at. Locals and tourists can enjoy this mall.
